The Strange case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde

By Robert Louis Stevenson

Synopsis

Gabriel John Utterson finds himself deeply worried for his close friend, Dr. Henry Jekyll, after receiving his latest will. He takes it upon himself to investigate the doctor as things get stranger and stranger. His investigations all seem to lead to the mysterious Mr. Hyde, but what could he have to do with his good friend?.
