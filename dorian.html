The picture of Dorian Gray

By Oscar Wilde

Synopsis

The picture of Dorian Gray follows the titular man, Dorian Gray. Basil Hallward, enamoured by him, paints a portrait of Dorian. When he finds that the portrait starts aging in his place he grows self centred and adventurous. Vain and beautiful he’s easily corrupted into a life of debauchery, romance and crime.
