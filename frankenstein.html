Frankenstein

By Mary Shelley

Synopsis

Victor Frankenstein fancies himself a God, arrogance leads him to believe that he can defy death itself. So he creates a creature out of human corpses to prove that he can do it. Perhaps he did it too well, as the creature grows resentful towards his creator and seeks revenge against him and his loved ones.
