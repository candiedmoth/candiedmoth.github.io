Dracula

By Brahm Stoker

Synopsis

Dracula is told through journal entries, letters and newspaper clippings. It follows a group of people realising they’re more connected than they thought and uncovering the mystery of the count. As the story progresses they become closer and delve into the world of vampire hunting.
